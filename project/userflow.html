Scenario: Beth works long hours at her job, and doesn’t have a lot of free time. She cares about her health and wellness, but doesn’t have time to make everything from scratch by herself. She likes to drink healthy, fresh juices that she can take with her throughout the day. She searches online for a healthy juices, and finds Juci, a juice company that specializes in fresh and interesting juices. She orders a variety of flavors so she can have a new one each day.

Actor: Beth

Motivator: Doesn't have a lot of free time.

Intention: Beth wants to have healthy juices that she can buy ready-to-drink.

Action: Beth is searching for different healthy juices that are not mass-manufactured. She finds Juci, a juice company that makes fresh juices using high-quality ingredients from scratch.

Resolution: Beth orders a variety of juices so she can have a different flavor every day.
